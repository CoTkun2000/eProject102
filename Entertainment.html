Thrilling Game

_________The Mind Bender_________
The Mind Bender is a coaster ride on J street, located in Merciado. Manufactured by Premier rides, the ride reaches a maximum height of 57 feet (17.5 m) and a maximum speed of 42.50 miles per hour (68.4 km/h). The coaster also features 2 inversions.
The Ride lasts for about 3 minutes and ensures an exhilarting experience!

The minimum age admitted on the Mind Bender is 8+.
_________The Splash Pit_________
One of the world’s coolest roller coasters is the Splash Pit at Merciado! it is not just any ordinary one; it is an underwater roller coaster. On its journey, this roller coaster enters into an underwater tunnel.
The ride is located on K street along with the Whirling Dervish and goes on for 2 minutes on a track that is 744 m long and reaches 35 m at one point. Looking for some excitement unheard of on roller coasters? The ride on Splash Pit is worth the experience!

The minimum age admitted on the Splash Pit is 5+.
_________The Whirling Dervish_________
The Whirling Dervish is one of the most popular rides at Merciado! Manufactured by Huss Rides, it was one of the first top spin flat rides built to feature water fountains, which spray riders at certain points in the ride cycle.
It is situated on K street at the park just beside the fountain. The Whirling Dervish was refurbished in 2007, with new settings placed onto the ride to improve ride-ability and make it even more interesting for you all.
The ride is surrounded by buildings resembling a traditional Arabic market town, while the ride itself is built into a pit.

The minimum age admitted on the Whirling Dervish is 8+.
_________The Wheel_________
The Wheel is a 550-foot tall (167.6 m) Ferris wheel located on H street in Merciado. The wheel, which began construction in 2009 and opened to customers in March 2012 is one of the world's tallest observation wheel. The Wheel features 24 spherical passenger cabins which can accommodate up to 35 passengers each, and is illuminated with dynamic multicolored LED lights nightly.

The minimum age admitted on the Wheel is 5+.
_________Twirl_________
Twirl is a new breed of thrill ride they call a Hypercoaster, which translates into utterly over the top, speed, height, and airtime. Can you feel the hype? The seating situation is minimal—no shoulder restraints, no sides on the cars – you’ll really feel like you’re just flying through space at insane speeds.
You’re going to have little time to gather up your courage, because that first hill is a staggering 233 feet high. As you crest the top, you’re so high up you can literally see all of Anclove City! But you won’t get to enjoy the view for long because almost immediately you’ll be thrust down a 215 ft drop.

The minimum age admitted on the Twirl is 5+.
_________The Monster's Eye_________
Merciado Park takes screaming to new heights when it opens its new Monster's Eye ride this spring. Riders on the Monster's Eye will be secured in an open-air two-person capsule before being launched nearly 360 feet into the sky at speeds of up to 62 mph!
Enjoy passing through gates while suspended so high in the sky. Monster's Eye will be located on G street near the twirl roller coaster and will be an extra charge amusement ride.

The Minimum age admitted on the Monster's Eye is 12+
